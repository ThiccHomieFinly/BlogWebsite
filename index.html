Rex Orange County

January 21th, 2020



This past week, me and my friends all went to Rex Orange County. He is a british singer who is very talented at multiple instruments. He is also a collabritor with one of my favorite artists Tyler The Creator, who I hope to see in concert on day. Overall, the expirience of seeing him was truly amazing. He intereacted with the crowd so well. One of my favorite parts of the whole expirience was when he told the crowd to put down there phones so then they could truly expirience the moment. He said if no one records, then this moment will be just between him and the crowd, which I really think is cool. Hopefully one day I could do something like what he does. This concert was truly a great expirience, and I think it got me a good start to my 2020. I really just liked being there with my friends and expiriencing great music for a night together. I also want to go to more concerts this year becuase I do truly enjoy going to them.
